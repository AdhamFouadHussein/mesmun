MESSAGE FROM OUR BOARD

Dina El Khateeb, MESMUN 2024 Organising Committee Head.

‘’To jump out of cliches, MESMUN is more than just a student activity that serves a specific goal. My first experience started at MESMUN 2021, heedless of what’s in store—the market, finances, academics, debates, everything—and by time it all came full circle by giving time and effort to learn more. Growing within this community enlightened me to serve my own achievements in a fulfilling way, building bridges and establishing an authentic work connection, anticipating the growth and opportunities resulting from a simple network leading to another. MESMUN this year aims to share a lifetime experience of unlocking everyone’s inner voice and expressing and fulfilling their own milestones.’’

Mariam Mohamed, MESMUN 2024 Co- Secretary General

‘’Reflecting on my MUN journey, gratitude overwhelms me for the community of passionate individuals and seekers of impact that became more than friends. The experiences enabled me to grow as a person in several aspects, making it an honour to give back to the community through  MESMUN—a well-crafted, intellectually stimulating experience. At MESMUN, we view knowledge as a catalyst for change, prioritising the manner in which the knowledge is shared. Our curated content delves into complex global issues through engaging presentations, interactive discussions, lively debates, diverse activities, and much more! Get ready for an unforgettable journey! Throughout the 8 years of MESMUN, we have maintained an environment that goes beyond a conference; it is a family that fosters lifelong bonds, and we are waiting for you to join us in our 9th year and the several more to come!’’

Ali Koheil, MESMUN 2024 Co-Secertary General.

‘’ I am thrilled to welcome you to the 9th generation of MESMUN! It's incredible to think that for 9 years, students have come together to work hard while having fun to achieve something amazing. A week, packed with activities that are both enjoyable and educational, and it's an experience I guarantee you will cherish for a lifetime.As someone who has been a part of MESMUN for 5 years, it holds a special place in my heart. For me, MESMUN has always been a safe space where I can express myself without fear of judgement. It has also helped me develop important soft skills, make new friends, and build connections. One of the things that I admire most about our Model UN is that it's inclusive. So if you're unsure about whether you're good enough to be a delegate (you probably are), I urge you to join us on this exciting journey and not miss out on this once-in-a-lifetime experience.’’
