Who are we?

Modern Education Schools Model United Nations is a seven-day United Nations simulation for high school students held annually in Modern Education Schools. At MESMUN, delegates gain insight into the workings of the United Nations and the dynamics of international relations by assuming the roles of world leaders and international decision-makers.

What’s New?

This year, MESMUN is giving the opportunity for international students to become delegates with the facilitation of some features, expanding their knowledge and culture with a variation of seven councils of a well-trained team. Adding endless opportunities for delegates to network, build their skills, and MORE!
